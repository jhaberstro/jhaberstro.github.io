Travel

We know this is prettttty much a destination wedding. We chose a venue about 4 hours from Jedd’s hometown, 2 hours from Chloe’s family, and 3000 miles from our California friends. You’re welcome, Californians.

That said, we know the effort and expense involved in a destination wedding. For that reason, we hereby absolve you of any guilt RSVPing “no” if the weekend away just isn’t in the cards. Seriously, we’ve been there and we get it. We love you and will celebrate with you in the future!

If you are planning to join us, there are a few ways to get to New Marlborough:

Drive

For both families, I90 is going to be your new friend! Pop on our Roadtrip Playlist from our cross country trip in 2016 and get rolling. Just make sure to enter the destination into your GPS before you hit New Marlborough as cell service is incredibly patchy near our venue. And by incredibly patchy, I mean nonexistant.

Fly

The closest airport to our venue is the Albany, NY airport. It’s a 45 minute drive to New Marlborough from Albany. You can rent a car at the airport, or take a pretty pricey Uber. Be sure to arrange a ride BACK to the airport, as there are no Ubers in New Marlborough (at time of writing, who knows what will happen by Oct 2020!)

Fly & Drive

Or, you can use our wedding as an excuse for an awesome vacation. Both New York City and Boston are about 3 hours from New Marlborough, and rental cars are plentiful in both cities. Why not take a road trip and visit a city while you’re here?
